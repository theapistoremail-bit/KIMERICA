Studio

We design visual systems that command attention.

KIMERICA is a creative agency focused on building digital presence through strategy, identity and immersive environments. We combine architecture, technology and conceptual art to create experiences that communicate authority, clarity and direction.

Our work exists at the intersection of luxury branding and emerging technology — where AI-generated proposals meet meticulously crafted visual execution. Every project begins with a single question: what does power look like in your market?

How We Work

A process built for
precision.

01
Discovery & Strategy

We start by understanding your territory.

Brand audit, competitive landscape, audience mapping, and positioning framework. We ask questions no one else asks, then we listen. The strategy phase defines the conceptual territory before a single visual decision is made.

Brand Audit Positioning Audience Research 2 Weeks
02
AI Proposal Generation

Claude translates your vision into creative direction.

Using your brief and our strategy outputs, Claude generates a precise creative direction proposal. Leonardo AI produces the first visual concepts. Meshy begins 3D asset exploration. You receive a complete AI-generated proposal — a real creative document, not a template.

Claude AI Leonardo AI Meshy 3D 48 Hours
03
Design & Visual System

Identity built to last decades, not trends.

From the AI proposal, our design team builds the full visual system: logotype, typography hierarchy, color architecture, iconography, motion principles, and every brand expression. Nothing decorative — every element is load-bearing.

Identity System Typography Motion 3–5 Weeks
04
3D & Immersive Build

We build the space your brand inhabits.

Three.js WebGL environments, real-time 3D brand objects from Meshy, AI-generated asset libraries, interactive experiences. The digital space becomes a territory with atmosphere, weight, and presence. Not decoration — architecture.

Three.js WebGL Real-time 3D 2–3 Weeks
05
Development & Launch

Deployed. Optimized. Commanding.

Full-stack development, performance optimization, cross-device refinement, and launch strategy execution. We don't hand over files and walk away — we deploy, monitor, and refine. Your brand goes live as a complete, functioning territory.

Full-Stack Dev Performance Launch Strategy 1–2 Weeks
06
Ongoing Partnership

We remain inside your brand ecosystem.

Retainer access, AI Lab requests via your dashboard, campaign extensions, 3D asset generation on demand. KIMERICA doesn't deliver projects — we build long-term creative infrastructures for brands that intend to lead.

AI Lab Access Retainer Ongoing
